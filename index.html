About Us

GreySpaces is an interior design company based in Pune who specialize in Home Decor and Offices. Our motto is: Giving you the place you deserve.

Services

What We Do.

At GreySpaces we work with home and office decor with the intention of making it presentable and look its best. We aren't just an interior design company but a gateway to your dreams.

Color Scheming

A beautiful home is made up of beautiful colors and that is first thing we make sure at GreySpaces. We make sure you get the perfect color schemes that highlight your personality and match perfectly with your requirements.

Accessories

We at GreySpaces make sure that you are provided with the best accesories for your homes. Accesories and showpieces that are much more than just decorations. We believe in using accesories to pass along a message to everyone who visits your place.

Office Services

We also provide corporates with office services like setting up the office, arranging the furniture and the color schemes ofcourse! We strongly beieve that a positive workplace means a positive workforce.

Furnishing

One of the main things that define a person's space is their furniture and at GreySpaces that is one of the things we give all of our attention to. We believe in getting you the best quality furniture.
